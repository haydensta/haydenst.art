Patient 00

sci-fi/comedy, 106 pages

David, a twenty-something stoner living in his grandmother's garage, and his

best friend Alex discover an adolescent test subject in an abandoned cryogenics

lab. David's comfortable life is uprooted as the three of them are hunted by police,

corporate conspirators, and the government, all of whom have a vested interest in Patient 00.
