I'm Hayden Startzell. This is my little place here.

I see myself as a writer and a filmmaker. I don't know which came first.

It's a chicken-egg situation because the first time I felt like

a writer was when I was two and got a notebook

for Christmas while I was being filmed.

Life's complicated like that.

I have extensive

experience in video editing and

photoshop tomfoolery and lifting heavy things but also do things like

art criticism and film and fiction writing and filmmaking. I'd like

to keep doing all of these probably until I die,

so if you'd like to do them with

me before that happens,

please reach out to me

at the address below:

hstartzell@gmail.com
