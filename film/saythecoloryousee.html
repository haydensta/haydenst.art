Say the color you see

film performance, 2023


Performed at AGX Film Collective in Waltham, MA, this multi-media piece tasks a
randomly chosen volunteer with interpreting several simple media elements all at
the same time. These include simple colors, identifying genders, and math problems.

Developed with guidance from Alison Folland


If you're interested in watching a recording

of this performance, send an email to:
